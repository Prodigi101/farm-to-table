Our story

We believe
food should taste
of somewhere

Farm to Table was founded by a former chef who was tired of the gap between what grows in the ground and what ends up on the plate. Somewhere between the farmer who woke up at 4am and the meal you're preparing, something usually goes wrong.

The chain is too long. The hands too many. The freshness — gone. We built this to fix that. One box. Twice weekly. Straight from Jamaican soil to your door

■
Regenerative only

Every partner farm builds, not depletes, topsoil health.

■
Zero food waste

Imperfect produce goes to partner businesses, never landfill.

■
Radical transparency

Every item is traceable to the field and the farmer who grew it.
